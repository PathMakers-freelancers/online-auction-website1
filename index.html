Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about bidding on BidNexus

How do I start bidding?

Simply create a free account, verify your email, and you're ready to start bidding! Browse our live auctions, select an item, and place your bid. You'll receive instant notifications if you're outbid.

Are all items authenticated?

Yes! Every item on BidNexus is professionally authenticated by certified experts before listing. We provide detailed authentication reports and certificates of authenticity.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption and processed through trusted payment gateways.

What if I win an auction?

Congratulations! You'll receive an email confirmation with payment instructions. Once payment is complete, we'll ship your item with full insurance and tracking. Items arrive within 5-7 business days.
