Common Questions

How accurate are your valuations?

Our valuations are conducted by certified specialists with an average of 15 years experience. We analyze recent auction results, condition reports, and market trends to provide estimates typically within 5-10% of the final sale price.

What happens if my item doesn't sell?

If an item fails to meet its reserve price, you have the option to re-list it in a future auction at a lower reserve or have it returned to you. No sale fees are charged for unsold items.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we have a dedicated logistics team that handles international shipping, customs documentation, and insurance. We offer white-glove delivery services for delicate and high-value items globally.

How long does the payment process take?

Once the buyer pays (typically within 48 hours of auction end), funds are held in secure escrow. Sellers generally receive payment within 5-7 business days after the item is delivered and verified.
